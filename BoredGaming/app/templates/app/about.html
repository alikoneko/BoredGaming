Find your perfect group.

Online, in person, at your local game store, it doesn't matter. Find people that want to play with you. Schedule meeting times, days, everything...and get an email reminder. (Calendar invites coming soon!) Your character profile has all your contact information!

Want to play with other people in real life? Are you in need of human contact and connection? Find people within 20 miles of your location to play with! BoredGaming.io is hub based so you can share your favorite places to game. If you are new to an area, you can see active gaming hubs where you can have a pick up game, making this perfect for the travelling TCG player and board gamer alike! Remember to practice basic internet safety. Never meet a stranger in real life. Consider playing at a local game store instead and take advantage of being at a hub.

Build your gaming community.

Board gaming, TCGs, and table top gaming is social. Part of being social is growing and expanding your community. Rate your experiences, share your favorite gaming spots, and favorite games. Build your profile so others can see how you like to game. Be social and build a community for all gamers!
